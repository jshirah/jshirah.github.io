Josie's Final Project

The link below is for Josie and Julissa's Extreme Tag. The game has 4 scenes, an splash scene, an instruction scene, a game scene, and a game end scene. The goal of the game is to use the mouse to control the avatar to avoid blocks moving across the screen. At the same time the user must "tag" the second bitmoji that appears on the screen.

Instructions from game: Blocks will move across the screen left to right and top to bottom. Use the mouse to move the avatar around avoiding the blue blocks. When a block hits your avatar it turns white and you lose 5 points. Tag Julissa's bitmoji by moving your mouse over the bitmoji to gain 15 points. Watch out the speed increases when Julissa is tagged. Game ends when Julissa is tagged 15 times or when all the blocks run out.
